About Minhad

Minhad M is a passionate educator and the visionary founder of ParamedicMallu, dedicated to revolutionizing paramedical education for students worldwide. With a Master's degree in Zoology from Mahatma Gandhi University and CSIR-UGC NET qualification in Life Sciences, he brings exceptional academic credentials to his teaching.

Teaching Excellence

Since 2014, Minhad has been specializing in medical entrance coaching, teaching crucial subjects including Biochemistry, Physiology, Anatomy, Microbiology, and Nutrition at various NEET-UG coaching centers. His expertise has helped countless students achieve their medical career dreams.

Mission & Vision

Through the ParamedicMallu platform established in 2023, Minhad creates accessible, high-quality educational content in Malayalam, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. As author of "The Blueprint of Biology," his mission is to empower the next generation of healthcare professionals with comprehensive, easy-to-understand learning resources.
